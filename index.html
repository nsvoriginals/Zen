Banking Systems

Zen is a banking application that aims to simplify financial management for its users. It offers a multi-currency account, allowing users to hold, manage, and transfer funds in various currencies all in one place. With instant money transfers, users can send and receive funds quickly and securely. Zen also provides physical and virtual debit cards for online and offline purchases, along with features like currency exchange at the best rates and shopping protection.
